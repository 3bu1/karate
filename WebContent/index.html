Welcome to the Kanka karate Academy!

The Kanka karate Academy is an amazing martial arts gym based 5 minutes away from Old Street Station and within close proximity to Angel, Moorgate and Liverpool Street tube/train Stations.

We work with everyone from complete beginners to professional fighters and pride ourselves on our knowledgeable instructors and fantastic students.

If you train with us, you'll gain some serious skills and some great friends along the way. Many talented people are involved in the team but, no matter what your ability, everybody treats each other with respect. We train hard together, not against each other.
